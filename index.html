About Strategize

Strategize was made to help each individual that has a vision or a dream to achieve it through using a flexible framework called the "Balanced Scorecard" introduced by Robert S. Kaplan and David P. Norton in the early 1990s. This framework has enabled us to build this application to fit professional and personal strategies, where it can allow an organization or even an individual to identify the desired directions according to their own vision and purpose and move forward from there through breaking it down to manageable, and well defined components.

This project was made as part of the Holberton School's Portfolio Project. The idea was in research for two weeks and the development of the project took another 2 weeks.

The project was developed by Abdelrahman Emara in the role of Fullstack Software Engineer.
